GeoNotes

Take notes anywhere.

Have you ever jotted something down, only to be later confused by your own note?

GeoNotes is a mobile-web app that allows note taking with geolocation information attached. Knowing where and when you took notes can make a significant difference when recalling and comprehending what you wrote!
